# Product Overview

## Mission

Help people decide what to watch next through trustworthy social recommendations and personal rating history.

## What MovieScore Is

A social media web platform for:

- Rating movies and TV series
- Writing reviews and reactions
- Following friends and creators
- Discovering recommendations based on social graph + taste profile

## Primary User Personas

- Casual watchers seeking quick recommendations
- Movie and series enthusiasts tracking everything they watch
- Friend groups sharing opinions and watchlists

## Product Principles

- Fast to record a rating
- Social activity should increase discovery quality
- Recommendations should be explainable (why this title)
- Trust and safety are first-class requirements
